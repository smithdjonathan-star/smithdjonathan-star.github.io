About

Strategy that connects priorities to impact.

Mobilis Works helps organizations design, launch, and implement strategic initiatives. We focus on the gap between statewide or national priorities and community-level impact — making sure that capital, programs, and policy actually land where they're intended and produce lasting results.

We work at the intersection of public policy and private investment, on problems that are too complex for any single actor but too important to leave unaddressed.

Approach

Where public and private interests intersect.

We work best on problems where stakeholders need to be aligned and where the hard part isn't knowing what to do — but figuring out how to get it done. Our work takes three forms:

Advise

We develop strategy that connects big-picture priorities to conditions on the ground.

Align

We build coalitions and shared agendas across the sectors, jurisdictions, and institutions that need to move together.

Activate

We stand up new programs from concept through implementation — because strategy is only valuable if it gets built.

Principal

Jonathan Smith

Jonathan Smith is the founder and principal of Mobilis Works LLC, a strategy and implementation practice focused on policy innovation, economic competitiveness, and civic capacity.

He most recently served in Governor Whitmer's Administration, where his work focused on guiding policy initiatives from design through implementation. His career spans senior roles in state government, on Capitol Hill, and in the private sector, including work in economic development policy, manufacturing strategy, and emerging technology.
